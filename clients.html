LTO Network solution

Business Process Management (BPM)

Business processes and agreements are essentially the way how promises between multiple parties are being executed.

We can visualize and structure these business rules as workflows.

Finite State Machine (FSM)

With LTO Network, those workflows can be expressed and automated as Finite State Machines. Such FSM workflows can instruct both machines and humans on the actions available to them based on the rules defined.

Click here for a video explanation.

LTO Network blockchain

By utilising blockchain technology, each party is able to have control over its own data and will be able to verify the actions of others, making cooperation secure and transparent.

Custodian & Supply Chain

LTO Network is working with the Dutch and Belgian governments for the waste transportation case as part of the EU tender won in early 2018. EU governments supervise the removal and transportation of waste to and from other EU countries and have to connect to multiple agencies to manage the operation. It currently relies on manual processes. Using LTO Network blockchain, the authority aims to free up human resources for other tasks.

LTO blockchain infrastructure provides an immutable way of verifying data about the weight of the shipment and the quota, making interactions among them trustless and secure.

CEMEX and LTO are also working on a supply chain case to remove the frictions of operational workflow of shipments in Latin America.

‘The unique thing is that party involved can keep his own system to communicate with the waste process.’ - Ben Zwartveld and Elliot Donata - Dutch Ministry of Infrastructure
Regulatory & Compliance

LTO Network is working with the Dutch government on automating data exchange for criminal cases (pitty crimes) and reducing the paperwork within the several stakeholders in the execution of the process.

After a successful pilot with LTO Network that entailed the streamlining of GDPR agreements between international insurance parties in which the finalized document was notarized through using Proof-of-Existence. The next step is integrating LTO Network to run and automate the processes on which DEKRA works together with other stakeholders.

'With LTO Network we can make our third party claim handling processes GDPR compliant' - Dekra
