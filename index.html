Not only the rules that you are agreeing upon in the contract, but also the consequences behind those rules. With our solution Live Contracts, we enable equality of rights for all parties involved.

Introducing Live Contracts @ Stanford CodeX

Timeline

Past, present and future

May 2014

Started in 2014 as platform to create, share and sign contracts in a digital way. After the success of our first company FIRM24.com, we believed that DTM (Document Transaction Management) would be the next big thing after ERP and CRM-systems.

May 2015

We released the first full version of the LegalThings platform. This version included the document and form builder, a document management system, advanced user and permission managment and signing.

Pushing the last commits while driving to our first client.

Jan 2016

For FIRM24 we created software to run the procedures behind an incorporation. Other customers were also interested in these digitized procedures. We did a small pivot, now focusing on digitizing the contracts as finite state machine, rather than just signing.

Sep 2016

We explored methods of providing proof of signed documents. Other parties relied on trust and certifications, we wanted a better way. We started using the Bitcoin blockchain for proof of existence. In this way, we silently introduced the blockchain with hundreds of businesses.

May 2017

We experimented with Ethereum Smart Contracts. We found out that it could only be used for self-enforcing agreements. Inherent constraints make them unusable for legally binding contracts.
Instead, we found a much better solution in using our FSM in combination with the blockchain.

Sep 2017

During the blockchain hackaton organized by the Dutch ministry of Justice, we demonstrated that a whole law can be digitized as FSM and be stored on the blockchian. This won us the first prize, which included the assigned to create the first digitized Dutch law on the blockchain.

6 Dec 2017

Our token pre-sale starts on the 6th of December. For 24 hours, the pre-sale is open only for members. You can register to be whitelisted and participate in this members-only pre-sale. After the whitelist pre-sale, we'll continue with a public pre-sale.

Dec 2017

We'll launch the LegalThings One platform as beta. All token holders will be able to use the platform. New content, like (live) model contracts will be added over the coming months.

Mar 2018

All tokens represent the full capacity of the LegalThings One platform. During this sale we'll sell licenses (represented by LTO tokens) for the platform.

Apr 2018

We'll publish the Live Contract syntax as open standards under the CC-BY-SA license. Software to run Live Contracts will be available under the MIT license. This will allow third parties to create applications to create and run Live Contracts, without relying on LegalThings One.

2019

To set precedence of the legality of data stored in the blockchain, we'll initiate trials in several countries. The following countries are candidates for these trials; the United Kingdom, the United States, India, Russia and the Netherlands.
