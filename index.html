Who uses LegalThings today?

Transfer Pricing documentation compliance regulation has put significant strain on the resources of companies, due to the complexity of diverse business models and the extensive geographical footprint of companies. LegalThings One digitizes the end-to-end process from creation-to-repository of transfer pricing documentation, by digitizing the repetitive parts of the documentation, workflow, and providing a robust search function to get your users close to their critical data; documents and progress (irrespective of device or geographical location). Finally, given that all steps and actions are recorded within the tool, accountabilities are transparent with clear visibility of progress.

Drafting legal documents clearly demands legal acumen, but there is a lot of work that can be standardized and automated. This eliminates a significant amount of "human errors" from the work of legal professionals, who in turn can focus more of their attention to the more intellectually demanding aspects of their work. This makes time spent by legal professionals more efficient. And efficient lawyers are more affordable and therefore more accessible.

Incorporating companies is highly standardized work. Deeds of incorporation of most companies are almost identical,but for a few minor details. Using electronic templates and workflows for the drafting of standardized deeds makes the incorporation process more efficient and therefore less costly for would-be entrepreneurs. In turn, notaries can focus more of their time to more demanding cases.

Real estate transactions typically involve a lot of legal documents (e.g., purchase agreement, notarial deed). Multiple parties (buyer, seller, real estate agents, public notary) are involved in the process, all relying on the same information. This requires a transparent process and trusted source of information. In this area a lot of work takes place in deviation of the “happy flow” : deals change, property gets sold during a rental period or real estate fails to comply with the agreement. This is where LegalThings One really shows its strengths, as it is flexible in its adaptation to reality.

Maintaining a workforce is a very paper-heavy affair. From the negotiation of the initial labour contract, to payrolling, renegotiating high-performers, maintaining a file for non-performing employees to the eventual dismissal, everything is heavily regulated and therefore needs to be documented error-free. Standardized workflows can help companies maintain an efficient HR control over large amounts of employees, and automate any legal steps to be taken over the life of the labour contract. LegalThings One software can provide a seamless and error-free solution for all.
