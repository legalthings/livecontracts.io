What is LegalThings One?

LegalThings One is a platform for running digitized workflows called Live Contracts. A Live Contract instructs humans and systems on what to do. These instructions are executed off-chain either manually or automatically. Nodes only facilitate, validate and verify the results.

Each Live Contract creates its own LEGALchain, a miniature private blockchain that is only shared with the participants involved. The platform has no master blockchain, instead LEGALchains anchor on public blockchains like Waves or Bitcoin. This approach make the platform horizontally scalable, sidestepping scalability issues seen in most blockchain technology.
